Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 14, 2025

Data Collection

Our SmartRoom app collects device IP addresses, sensor data (door and drawer status), and images captured from the ESP32-CAM. This data is used only to operate the SmartRoom features.

Data Usage

The collected data is used to display real-time room status, send push notifications, and allow image capture and video streaming.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with any third parties. However, we use Firebase for notifications and cloud data storage.

Security

All data is transmitted over secure protocols (HTTPS) where possible. We take reasonable measures to protect your information.

User Rights

You can request deletion of your data by contacting us at: support@smartroom.com

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@smartroom.com
